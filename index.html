What we do

Cyber Green is an Australian cybersecurity firm that specializes in advice, information technology systems auditing and remediation, with a focus on helping businesses in the disability, healthcare and aged care sectors conform to the Australian privacy principles.

Our 40-point cybersecurity audit is a comprehensive evaluation of an organization's cybersecurity posture, covering all the essential areas of security, including access controls, incident response, data loss prevention, and regulatory compliance. Our audit report highlights the strengths and weaknesses of an organization's security posture and provides recommendations for improvement.

About

Our founders, Jon Paior and Ryan Verner, have extensive experience in the field of cybersecurity and a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced by organizations in the disability sector.

Our target audience is Australian large SME and smaller corporate businesses with more than 30 staff.

Our goal is to help organizations in this sector secure their sensitive data and protect their clients, employees, and assets from cyber threats.

We understand the importance of cost-effective solutions, which is why our cybersecurity audit services are tailored to meet the specific needs and budget of each client. We work with our clients to develop a plan of action that takes into account their current security posture, budget, and business goals. Our mission is to provide top-notch cybersecurity services that help our clients achieve a high level of security and peace of mind.

At Cyber Green, we believe that cybersecurity is not just about technology, but about people. That is why we strive to educate and empower our clients to make informed decisions about their cybersecurity posture. We are committed to providing outstanding customer service and to building long-lasting relationships with our clients.

If you're looking for a trusted partner to help you secure your organization's sensitive data and protect your clients, employees, and assets from cyber threats, look no further than Cyber Green.
